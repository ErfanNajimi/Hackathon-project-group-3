Cyber Safety Tips

Password

  • Do not use the same username and password for all websites
  • Use a password with 15 to 20 characters including letters and numbers
  • Do not share your password to other people
  • Use a password manager
  • Use multi-factor authentication

Malware

  • Regularly update antivirus software
  • Exercise caution when downloading and opening files
  • Practice safe internet browsing
  • Enable firewall protection
  • Regularly backup your data

Cyberbullying

  • Review your privacy settings
  • Understand why bullies do it
  • Avoid responding
  • Do not re-read offensive comments
  • Open communication within your family

Phishing

  • Keep informed about phishing techniques
  • Never provide personal information from an email request
  • Be Wary of Pop-Ups
  • Install an Anti-Phishing Toolbar
  • Think before you click!
